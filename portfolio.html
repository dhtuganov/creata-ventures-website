NMT (NeuroMindTherapy)

First in Kazakhstan & CIS: scientifically validated psychotherapy platform with EEG neuro-monitoring. Patented methodology backed by PhD research.

The Problem

Our Solution

NeuroMindTherapy combines evidence-based psychotherapy (CBT, Gestalt) with portable EEG headsets to provide:
